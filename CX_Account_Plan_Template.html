Legend / Key

Definitions used across the Stakeholder Matrix and Engagement Plan.

Buying Influence (BI)

Economic Buyer (EB) · Controls budget; final say on spend.
User Buyer (UB) · Day-to-day user of our service; judges performance directly.
Technical Buyer (TB) · Gatekeeper — can rule us out, can't say yes alone.
Coach (C) · Internal advocate inside the client org.
Gatekeeper (G) · Controls access to other buyers.

Level of Influence (LI)

High · Drives decisions and shapes outcomes.
Moderate · Has meaningful input.
Low · Limited input.
None · No influence on the decision.

Decision Authority (DA)

Final Decision-Maker · Signs off.
Key Decision Influencer · Their opinion carries weight.
Recommender · Suggests, doesn't decide.
Low / No Authority · Minimal sway over outcome.

Strategic Role (SR)

Sponsor · Champions Pritchard internally.
Key Player · Important but not advocating.
Coach · Helps us navigate the org.
Anti-Sponsor · Working against us.

Quality of Relationship (QR)

Trusted Ally · Strong, reciprocal.
Supportive · Generally positive.
Neutral · No meaningful relationship yet.
Skeptical · Some doubt or distance.
Adversarial · Active friction.

How They View Us (HV)

5 — Organizational Partner · Strategic, embedded.
4 — Business Partner · Trusted, beyond a vendor.
3 — Exceeds Expectations · Strong vendor.
2 — Meets Expectations · Acceptable vendor.
1 — Does Not Meet · At risk.

Refresh stakeholder matrix and VOC after every QBR. Update engagement touchpoints monthly. The "last reviewed" field on Overview is your audit trail.
